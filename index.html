What makes TradePulse different from other trading journals? expand_more

TradePulse goes beyond basic trade logging with institutional-grade analytics, AI-powered insights, and professional risk management tools. Unlike basic journals, we provide actionable feedback to improve your trading strategy, not just record keeping.

Which brokers do you integrate with? expand_more

We support all major brokers including Interactive Brokers, TD Ameritrade, E*TRADE, Fidelity, Charles Schwab, Robinhood, and more. Our platform also supports manual import for brokers we don't yet integrate with directly.

Is my trading data secure? expand_more

Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption for all data transfers and storage. Your trading data is never shared or sold to third parties. We also offer two-factor authentication for added security.

Can I try before I buy? expand_more
